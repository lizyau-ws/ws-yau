Background

Whenever I mention candle making, people often think it is by pouring melt wax into a mold only. The art of candle making is actually more than that. The wax materials, condition of room temperature, precise temperature controls in regards of color mixing, pouring and kneading or even the choice of candle wick and pigments, every tiny detail affects the presentation of the candle at the end. I happened to study candle making course under Japan Candle Association in July 2020. During the course, I have learned so much - creativity, patience, flexibility and the willingness to keep exploring and experiement different elements - in regards of this craftmanship
