Liz Yau

Hey there! I'm Liz Yau,
an UX student at De Haagse Hogeschool



I have always believe in the power of design which exists at all levels of our society: from architecture to data visualization, from bio-design to urban planning, from advertising to every single product. Design has an inherent restorative ability and we, as designers, can bring concepts and objects into the existence that can carry different messages and raise awareness towards certain issue, or even to strengthen the weakening bonds between people and social environments.


Before my UX program in The, my major was Print Media in Hong Kong Design Institute. Even though people would consider it as less important nowadays, my study journey there makes me truly realize and has built up my awareness that, it doesn't matter of which design field it is or how tiny of each decision we need to making in the process, every tiny bit of it can already create different consequences and impression for the user.


All these different experience has lead me to where I am at at this moment, to develop user-friendly and communicative experiences and services to create value for enterprises, society and individual users.
